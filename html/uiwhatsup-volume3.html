Hawkeyes, we are in the homestretch of the semester. I am sure many of you are feeling the stress of exams, papers, and finals. I encourage you to use the resources we have available to help you stay on track for a strong finish. Your Division of Student Life staff is here to support you at all times. Don't forget that counseling services, stress management assistance, and many group fitness classes are available to you.
 

Thank you, Veterans


As we celebrated those who serve and protect our country this Veterans' Day, I hope you took a moment to visit the display of over 5,500 flags that honor veterans with ties to UI. The display, which formed an "I," was on the front lawn of Old Capitol.
 
We also honored six members of the armed forces for exception service to their country with a celebration on Wednesday, November 15. The Division of Student Life was proud to co-sponsor this event and recognize Hawkeyes who have honorably served in the military.

Holiday Travel Plans


With final exams just around the corner, we are preparing for two big holiday closings.  UI's Thanksgiving recess is November 19-26. Please be aware that the residence hall contract dining closes on November 17 and will reopen on November 27 when classes resume. Have a safe and relaxing break!
 
The last day of classes for the Fall 2017 semester is December 8, and final exams begin on December 11. We will host our fall commencement ceremony on December 14, and residence hall dining centers will close on December 15. All students who live in residence halls must be out of their rooms by December 16, when halls close for the semester. Residence halls will re-open on January 13 and classes for Spring 2018 begin on January 16.
 
If you have any questions about policies and procedures regarding housing or dining with the holiday schedule, please reach out to housing@uiowa.edu.
 

Airport Shuttles


If you will be traveling via air for any of the upcoming breaks, please be aware of the free shuttle transportation UI provides to and from Eastern Iowa Airport. Schedules can be found at https://uisg.uiowa.edu/airport-shuttle.
