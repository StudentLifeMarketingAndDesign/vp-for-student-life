Happy Tuesday, Hawkeyes!

If we haven't met yet, we soon will. I am proudly going into my third month serving you as the Vice President for Student Life and am delivering on my promise to communicate with you on a regular basis. I want you to know what is happening in the Division of Student Life. We are a staff of people dedicated to you, the most important assets on our campus, and I want you to know who we are.
 

So, what can you expect for UI What's Up?

Get to know other students and staff. Each month we will ask two people to answer the same questions. This will be a brief, fun way for us to get to know each other better. This month I've shared a few of my secrets with you, but going forward you will see students and staff from the Division of Student Life. 

#UIWHATSUP. Communication is a two-way street, and I want to hear from and showcase YOU! If you have a story or a photo you want to share with me, use the hashtag #UIWHATSUP on Twitter and Instagram. Maybe I'll even share some of your photos next month!

News and updates. My goal is to provide you with timely updates about things happening on campus - events, speakers, volunteer opportunities, and student activities. While After Class is a great resource, I will love highlighting some of our pride points each month.

Getting to know me

As promised, here is a little peek into who I am.

1. The coolest place I've ever traveled is... Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It was fun to visit a different country, immerse myself in the culture, and learn more about how student affairs functions on their college campuses. 

2. I would spend my last dollar on... research to identify a cure for cancer. My mom is a cancer survivor yet many families including my own, have lost loved ones to this dreadful disease. 

3. The best kept secret in Iowa City is... all the restaurants I haven't had an opportunity to visit. I've been told there's more than Basta here and I'm ready to experience all of them. Anyone want to show me around??? 

4. My favorite things to do are... spending Saturdays in a college football stadium (Kinnick is amazing) or watching college football on TV. I record at least three games every Saturday and can talk football with the best of them! I also love going to the movies on Sundays. If you enjoy movies, let's share our analysis of the best and not-so-great films.

5. My dream for the University of Iowa is... we continue to offer the best higher education experience - in and out of the classroom - in the country!

Thank you for inviting me on this journey with you. Please know that I am here to serve you and my door is always open.
